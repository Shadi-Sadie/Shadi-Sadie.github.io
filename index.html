About

I'm a health economist focused on evaluating policy impacts and value for money in healthcare. My recent work examines the Affordable Care Act's Medicaid expansion using interaction‑weighted event studies, and analyzes health outcomes and expenditure patterns in MEPS with survey‑weighted models and subgroup analysis.

Method interests: identification in staggered adoption settings, survey design & variance estimation, budget impact modeling, and decision‑useful evidence generation for payers and providers.
